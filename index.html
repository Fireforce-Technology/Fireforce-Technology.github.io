Pollutionless & efficient biomass combustion processes

The Fireforce clean combustion process produces heat energy efficiently with ultra-low gaseous or particles pollutant emissions. It allows notably wood pellets or wood chips clean combustion and opens the way to cleaner, easier and cheaper combined heat and power generation (CHP) solutions from wood.
